What we do

Garden

We provide once-off or year round garden maintenance services. We can take care of your grass cutting, hedge trimming, strimming, weed control and planting needs. Maybe you need some hard landscaping or fencing. We do that too! All projects big and small considered!!

Flat-Pack Assembly

Does flat-pack assembly send you into a spin? Is the thought of it alone enough to cause you stress? Have no fear! We will assemble all your flat-packs big and small. For larger items from local stores we can collect the product for you and then assemble at a time that suits you. Ordering online? No problem just let us know when the product is being delivered!

Pressure Washing & Cleaning

We can pressure wash driveways, yards and external walls, clean windows and clear roof gutters. You will need to provide an external water source (tap). We will do the rest!

Interior Painting and Decorating

We will paint internal doors, walls and ceilings, hang wallpaper, erect shelving, hang pictures and carry out many typical wear and tear repairs that occur around the home. Have something in mind but do not see it listed here. Just get in touch and we will discuss your particular requirements.
